# Memory Keeper - User Guide

## Welcome!

Welcome to Memory Keeper. This application helps you record your valuable life stories for your family to cherish forever.

## For Storytellers (The "Elderly" View)

### How to Start

1. **Open the Website**: Ask your family member to set up the tablet or computer.
2. **Press the Microphone**: Look for the big red microphone button.
3. **Start Talking**: The AI will ask you a question like "What was your childhood home like?". Just answer naturally.
4. **Pause Anytime**: Press the square "Stop" button if you need a break.

### Tips

- Speak clearly and at your own pace.
- It's okay to pause or correct yourself.
- If you don't want to answer a question, just say "Skip".

---

## For Family Admins

### Dashboard Overview

- **Stats**: See how many hours have been recorded.
- **Timeline**: View the extracted life events on a visual map.
- **Export**: Generate a PDF storybook from the 'Settings' menu.

### Managing Topics

1. Go to **Dashboard > Topics**.
2. Select areas you want the AI to focus on (e.g., "Wartime Experience", "Cooking").
